About Beta Egypt
Awesome Since 1993

Beta Egypt for Urban Development is one of the fastest growing Real Estate developers in the Egyptian market since 1993. Headquartered in Heliopolis, Cairo – Beta Egypt has investments worth 6 billion Egyptian pounds with landmarks created through fully holistic and blissful communities. Beta Egypt focuses on creating high-end housing communities that cater to meet Egypt’s emergent market demand. Beta Egypt celebrates 28 years of successful operations that have led to the creation of gated communities with over 3000 families living within its sustained communities in 6th of October and Obour City. Beta Egypt focuses on building happy communities that redefine living standards, by putting your desires and aspirations first.
